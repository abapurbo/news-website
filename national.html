National Health Initiative Launched

A nationwide health initiative has been launched to provide free medical checkups and preventive care in rural areas of Bangladesh. Mobile medical teams will visit villages, offering screenings, vaccinations, and health education. The program aims to reduce disease prevalence, improve early detection of health issues, and raise awareness about hygiene and nutrition. Health officials stressed that community participation and collaboration with local organizations are key to the program’s success, ensuring accessible healthcare for all citizens.
